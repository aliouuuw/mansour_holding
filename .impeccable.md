# Design Context — Mansour Holding

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Affluent clientele in Dakar, Senegal — business owners, executives, diaspora returning home
- **Secondary**: Holding company stakeholders and partners
- **Context**: Browsing on mobile (60%+) and desktop, often on the go, comparing with international luxury brands

## Use Cases
1. Discover Mansour Holding and its portfolio of businesses
2. Browse and inquire about premium vehicles (Mansour Motors)
3. Contact the company for business partnerships or vehicle purchases
4. Access the professional dashboard (Espace Pro)

## Brand Personality & Tone
- **Sovereign luxury** — not flashy, but authoritative and refined
- **Cinematic editorial** — like a high-end magazine spread, not a SaaS landing page
- **West African excellence** — grounded in Dakar, projecting international standards
- **Confident restraint** — gold accents used sparingly for maximum impact

## Design Direction: "Sovereign Luxury"
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (emotional/serif) + Plus Jakarta Sans (precision/sans)
- **Colors**: Varied — dark cinematic heroes, warm light body sections, gold accents throughout
- **Texture**: Noise overlays, cinematic vignettes, radial gradients
- **Motion**: Smooth scroll (Lenis), parallax, reveal-on-scroll, magnetic hover
- **Photography**: Full-bleed hero images with cinematic overlays
- **Layout**: Editorial asymmetry, generous whitespace, typography-driven hierarchy

## Theme Strategy
- **Heroes**: Always dark/cinematic — noir-950 with photography and gold accents
- **Body sections**: Warm light — surface-dim (#F9F9F7), white cards, noir-950 text
- **Footer**: Always dark — noir-950, anchors the page
- **Navbar**: Adapts — transparent on dark heroes, white/blurred on scroll & light sections
- **Gold on light**: Use gold-600/700 for better contrast (not gold-400)
- **Gold on dark**: Use gold-400 for glow and prominence

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Generic SaaS card grids
- Bounce/elastic easing
- Gradient text for impact
- Cards inside cards
- Same spacing everywhere
- Pure white (#fff) backgrounds — use warm off-whites
- Pure black text on light — use noir-950 (softer)

## Audit Findings (2026-03-16)
- PublicVehicles uses light theme — jarring transition from dark luxury pages
- MansourMotorsLanding hero has no background image — feels empty
- Duplicate `cn` utility in LandingPage (also in lib/utils)
- LandingPage has inline Footer instead of shared PublicFooter
- Duplicate CarProfile icon in Motors services
- PublicVehicleDetail back links point to `/vehicules` instead of `/mansour-motors/vehicules`
- PublicNavbar only has 2 links — too sparse for a holding company
- PublicFooter nav links are all dead (`#`)
- Section order in LandingPage: About before Portfolio (should be reversed)
- No breadcrumb navigation on sub-pages
- `hover-trigger` class used but never defined
- `bg-gradient-radial` used but not a standard Tailwind utility
