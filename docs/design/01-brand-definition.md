### 4.3 Typography Rules

- **Headings on light**: `noir-950` (near-black)
- **Headings on dark**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white)
- **Body text**: `noir-600` on light, `noir-300` on dark
- **Prices / KPI values**: Always `font-bold` or `font-extrabold`, `noir-950`
- **Gold text**: Only for premium labels, active nav, signature moments
- **Uppercase tracking**: Overlines and nav section headers use `uppercase tracking-widest`
- **No italic** in UI â€” use weight or gold color for emphasis
- **Numbers**: Use `tabular-nums` for aligned columns

## 7. Photography & Imagery

### Style Guidelines
- **Vehicles**: Studio-quality, dramatic lighting, dark or neutral backgrounds preferred
- **Mood**: Aspirational, cinematic â€” think luxury car commercial, not used-car lot
- **People**: Confident, well-dressed, professional Senegalese context
- **Properties**: Architectural, clean lines, golden hour or twilight
- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9 (hero), 4:3 (cards), 1:1 (avatars)

### Image Treatment
- **Slight desaturation** for consistency across different source images
- **Dark overlay gradients** on hero images for text readability
- **No rounded corners on hero images** â€” full-bleed or sharp edges
- Card images: `rounded-lg` maximum, never `rounded-2xl`

### Placeholder Strategy
- Use Unsplash for development â€” prefer dark/dramatic vehicle shots
- Production: Self-hosted on S3-compatible storage (MinIO/R2)
- Always provide `alt` text in French
- Lazy load all images below the fold
